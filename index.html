Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is my document uploaded to a server?

Text extraction happens locally in your browser using OCR. Only the extracted text is sent to the AI model for explanation, summary, or translation.

Which languages are supported?

You can translate results into any major world language supported by the AI model, including Spanish, Hindi, Mandarin, Arabic, French, and more.

Do I need to create an account?

No. AccessAI works entirely in your browser with no sign-up required. Your history is saved locally on your device.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload common image formats like JPG, PNG, and WEBP — photos, screenshots, or scans all work.

Is AccessAI free?

Yes, AccessAI is completely free to use.
